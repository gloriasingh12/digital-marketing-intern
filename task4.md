# Brand Reputation Management (BRM) Strategy
**Project:** Online Image & Sentiment Control (Aditya Tripathi / Salaam Namaste)
**Objective:** Building trust and managing digital perception.

---

## 1. Monitoring Strategy (Kaise pata chalega log kya bol rahe hain?)
* **Google Alerts:** "Aditya Tripathi" aur "Salaam Namaste Radio" ke liye alerts set karna taaki har naye mention ka pata chale.
* **Social Listening:** Twitter aur LinkedIn par brand mentions aur keywords track karna.
* **Review Tracking:** App stores, Google Business, aur social media comments ko daily monitor karna.

## 2. Handling Negative Feedback (Aag kaise bujhayein?)
* **The 24-Hour Rule:** Har negative review ka jawab 24 ghante ke andar dena.
* **Professional Tone:** Gali ya gusse ka jawab hamesha empathy aur solution ke saath dena.
* **Example Response:** *"Hum maafi chahte hain ki aapko ye problem hui. Kya hum ise thik karne ke liye aapse DM mein baat kar sakte hain?"*
* **Move Offline:** Public debate se bachne ke liye customer ko support email ya DM par le jana.

## 3. Building Positive Sentiment (Hawa kaise banayein?)
* **User-Generated Content (UGC):** Fans aur students ko apna experience share karne ke liye encourage karna.
* **Testimonials:** Achhe feedback ko highlight karke LinkedIn aur website par post karna.
* **Thought Leadership:** Valuable content share karna (jaise ye 50+ tasks) taaki authority bane.

## 4. Recommended Tools
| Tool | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Google Alerts** | Free brand monitoring across the web. |
| **Hootsuite / Buffer** | Managing social media mentions in one place. |
| **Trustpilot** | Collecting and displaying verified customer reviews. |
| **Canva** | Creating positive review cards for social sharing. |
