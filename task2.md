# Task 54: Content Marketing Strategy Document
**Brand/Project:** Aditya Tripathi's Tech Portfolio & Salaam Namaste Community Radio
**Goal:** Establish thought leadership in Cloud, AI, and DevOps.

---

## 1. Target Audience (Kise Target Kar Rahe Hain?)
* **Primary:** Technical Recruiters & HR Managers (Looking for DevOps/Cloud talent).
* **Secondary:** Junior Developers & Students (Looking for tutorials/guidance).
* **Tertiary:** Local Community (Salaam Namaste Radio listeners for tech awareness).

## 2. Content Pillars (Main Topics)
* **The "How-To" Hub:** Practical tutorials on AWS, Kubernetes, and Python.
* **Innovation Insights:** Deep dives into AI, Blockchain, and Web3 trends.
* **Behind the Code:** Personal journey, internship experiences, and SOIT Expo updates.

## 3. Blog/Article Topics (Next 3 Months)
1.  **Month 1:** "Moving from Local to Cloud: A Beginner's Guide to AWS S3."
2.  **Month 2:** "Why DevSecOps is the Future of Software Security."
3.  **Month 3:** "How Community Radio is Using AI to Reach Rural India."

## 4. Promotion Channels (Kahan Share Karenge?)
| Channel | Purpose | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional networking, sharing certificates, and long-form articles. | 3x / Week |
| **GitHub** | Hosting code, detailed READMEs, and project documentation. | Daily Commits |
| **Medium/Hashnode**| Technical blogging and deep-dive tutorials. | 1x / Month |
| **Salaam Namaste** | Audio snippets and tech talk shows. | Weekly |

## 5. Key Performance Indicators (Success Kaise Napenge?)
* **Reach:** 500+ LinkedIn profile views per month.
* **Engagement:** At least 20+ stars/forks on the Task Repository.
* **Conversion:** At least 3 interview calls or professional networking requests.
